Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

This privacy policy explains how goxclaim.com ("the Site"), operated by Christofer Thorén ("I", "me"), collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

What I collect

When you use the contact form on this site, I collect:

  • Email address — so I can respond to your inquiry
  • Creditor number, username, or old email — if you provide it, to check your claim status
  • Message content — whatever you choose to share about your situation

I do not use cookies for tracking. I do not use analytics tools. The site is hosted on GitHub Pages, which may collect standard server logs (IP addresses, access times) as part of normal web hosting operations. I do not have access to these logs.

How I use your information

Your information is used solely for:

  • Responding to your inquiry
  • Assessing whether you have a valid Mt. Gox claim
  • Communicating with you about the recovery process if you choose to proceed

I do not sell, share, or transfer your personal information to third parties. I do not use your information for marketing purposes beyond responding to your direct inquiry.

Form submissions

Contact form submissions are processed through Formspree, a third-party form handling service. Formspree stores your submission data on their servers. You can review Formspree's privacy policy at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

Data retention

I retain your contact information and correspondence for as long as needed to provide services related to your claim, or until you ask me to delete it. If you contact me and decide not to proceed, I delete your information within 30 days of our last communication.

Your rights

Under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data I hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email me at info@goxclaim.com.

Security

I take reasonable measures to protect your personal information. All communications are handled through encrypted email. The site is served over HTTPS. However, no method of electronic transmission is 100% secure, and I cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to this policy

I may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
